Fake diamond tools have 15 times less durability than diamond tools and FD armor has circa 5 times less durability
than diamond armor. The durability is the only diffrence between fake diamond and diamond stuff.

Fake diamonds drop from skeletons and creepers to begin with.
50% drop chance for skeletons and 70% drop chance for creepers.
skeletons and creepers drop 1 fake diamond and have a 25% chance to drop 2.

Blank Tablet - will be used to craft the other tablets.
Destruction Tablet - will destroy picked up items, specified by tokens. - mostly implemented.

Water Tablet - will place water when right clicked. - mostly implemented.
Lava Tablet - will place lava when right clicked. - mostly implemented.
Cobblestone Tablet - will place cobblestone when right clicked. - mostly implemented.
Dirt Tablet - will place dirt when right clicked. - mostly implemented.
Gravel Tablet - will place gravel when right clicked. - mostly implemented.
Reversing Tablet - will be used to reverse craft items, but not using a crafting table. - partly implemented.
Time Tablet - will be used fast forward the time. - mostly implemented.
Reverse Time Tablet - will be used to rewind the time. - partly implemented.
Growth Tablet - will cause plants right clicked on to grow faster, somewhat like bonemeal. - partly implemented.
Flight Tablet - will grant the player creative flight. - partly implemented.
