Confirmed Features:
-------------------

Lesser Essence, Essence and Greater Essence.

Lesser Essence Ore, Essence Ore and Greater Essence Ore. 

Tools made with Lesser Essence.

Need Diamond Pickaxe to mine Lesser Essence Ore.

Need Lesser Essence Pickaxe to mine Essence Ore.

Tools and Armor made with Essence. 

Need Essence Pickaxe to mine Greater Essence Ore.

Tools and Armor made with Greater Essence.

Seed Catalyst made with 1 Essence, 4 Coal Fragments, 4 Dirt.

Seeds for all 3 tiers, need 1 Seed, 4 Essence and 4 Seed Catalyst.
