Confirmed Features:
-------------------

Base Tablet - will be used to craft the other tablets.

Water Tablet - will place water when right clicked, might have durability. 

Lava Tablet - will place lava when right clicked, might have durability.

Cobblestone Tablet - will place cobblestone when right clicked, might have durability.

Reversing Tablet - will be used to reverse craft items, but not using a crafting table, might have durability. 

Day Tablet - will be used to switch to the begining of the day, might have durability.

Night Tablet - will be used to switch to the begining of the night, might have durability.
